Technology

Our wish for the technology portion of our program is to inform our audience about the basic use of electronics like computers and smartphones. We also want to provide instruction about using Microsoft Office, managing and navigating the Internet, and internet safety.

Financial Literacy

In the financial literacy aspect of our organization, we hope to educate students about crucial abilities needed to succeed in the 21st century workforce. We will teach them basic financial skills such as managing money wisely, making knowledgeable decisions when it comes to investments, and filing tax returns.

Career Advice

The career advice section of our program is to instruct students across the nation by equipping them with the necessary skills to make educated choices when deciding their career paths. We want to ensure that all students are content with the lifelong occupations that they choose for themselves and are able to create successful lives out of them.

Our Mission

Greatness through Guidance hopes to give all American students the ability to succeed by providing counseling in digital literacy, financial literacy, and career advice through partnering with local organizations.

Greatness through Guidance is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt corporation, and we greatly appreciate any donations to our cause.
