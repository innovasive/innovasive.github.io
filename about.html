About Greatness through Guidance

Greatness through Guidance is a non-profit organization that provides curriculums and counseling in financial literacy, digital literacy, and career advice to American students who are not educated in these subjects. We plan to do so by conducting educational classes in Philadelphia, PA. As we grow, our movement will reach from sea to shining sea.
